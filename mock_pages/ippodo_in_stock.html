Ikuyo is a medium-bodied matcha with a refreshing bitterness that is slightly more noticeable than its sweetness and umami.

As usucha, it is a good option for everyday teatime, served straight and with cookies, cakes, or other sweets.

One of our top recommendations for a matcha latte, Ikuyo has slight astringency that pairs well with the milk, and its bright green color makes for a photogenic beverage.

Best before date: 180 days from when it is freshly packaged in Japan

Serving size: Usucha: 2g (1 tsp.) | Latte: 3g (1.5 tsp.)

Product Details

Ikuyo is a balanced matcha with astringency, umami, and sweetness in proportion. Its medium-light body is joined by a gentle, but bright aroma, like sun-dried straw.
